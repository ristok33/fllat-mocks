Description

Flex Plus is one of our budget-friendly options. It has a single-size bed, smart TV, and is separated from the rest of the suite with a smart bookcase system. It is only yours, in which you can enjoy the privacy together with quality furniture at a very reasonable price. It is significant to highlight that the bookcase system does not fully insulate the area. Flex Plus is quite on demand for a couple of reasons. It is the best option in terms of price/performance ratio. You pay less and enjoy all the benefits of living in a luxury high rise in the heart of the city.

Neighbourhood/Building: Our Canary Wharf Residence is one of the most luxurious highrise buildings located in one of the highest-end neighbourhoods in London. This residence offers more than you can imagine and will upgrade your lifestyle once you step in. Find comfort in a super luxury apartment with a swimming pool, gym, and concierge. Amazing view, right above the water. Our residence is located right across the underground entrance at Canary Wharf station. It takes only 5 stops to arrive in Central London, less than a 10-minute ride.
